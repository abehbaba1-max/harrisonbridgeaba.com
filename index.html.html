What We Offer

ABA therapy that meets your child where they are

Every service delivered in your child's natural environment by compassionate, BCBA-supervised clinicians.

Primary Service

In-Home ABA Therapy

We bring individualized, naturalistic ABA directly into your home — targeting communication, daily living skills, social behaviors, and behavior reduction in the environment your child knows best. Sessions are built around your family's schedule.

Academic & Social

School-Based Support

Our clinicians collaborate with teachers, aides, and IEP teams to ensure ABA strategies are consistent between home and school — so your child gets support everywhere they learn.

Real-World Skills

Community-Based Services

Grocery stores, parks, restaurants, recreational centers — we work in the real world so your child builds the independence that matters most for daily life outside the home.

Early Childhood

Daycare Consultation

We partner with daycare and early learning programs — providing on-site behavioral support, staff training, and consultation so your child's entire day is structured and consistent.

Foundation of Care

Behavioral Assessments

Before treatment begins, our BCBAs conduct comprehensive functional behavior assessments and skills evaluations to understand your child's strengths, priorities, and the drivers behind challenging behaviors.

Family Empowerment

Caregiver Training

We give parents and caregivers the tools, strategies, and confidence to support their child's growth around the clock — because progress in therapy only sticks when it carries into everyday life.

Our Story

Built for families. Led by clinicians.

Harrison Bridge ABA was founded with one mission: give North Carolina families access to the gold standard of ABA — without ever leaving their world to get it.

Our Mission

We exist to bridge the gap between where a child is today and where they are capable of going. Through the science of Applied Behavior Analysis — delivered with genuine warmth, deep clinical expertise, and unwavering commitment to each family — we help children across North Carolina reach milestones that matter.

What We Believe

Our Values

01

Evidence-Based Practice

Every decision is grounded in the science of behavior analysis. Proven, research-backed methods — with data measuring progress and guiding every adjustment we make.

02

Family as Partners

Parents aren't bystanders — they are essential members of the treatment team. We collaborate, communicate openly, and train families to sustain progress around the clock.

03

Child-Centered Always

Every plan is individualized. We start by understanding each child's strengths, interests, and goals — building therapy that feels natural, not clinical or forced.

04

Compassion Over Everything

Science without heart isn't enough. Every session, every home, every school — we show up with genuine warmth because children thrive when they feel safe, seen, and valued.

05

Full Transparency

Families deserve to know exactly what we're doing and why. We share data, explain goals clearly, and communicate openly at every stage of treatment.

06

Committed to Access

North Carolina families deserve access to high-quality ABA. We are committed to making services accessible through Medicaid and managed care insurance plans.

What does coverage include?

Most Medicaid and managed care plans cover ABA therapy when there is a qualifying diagnosis such as Autism Spectrum Disorder and a physician recommendation. Coverage typically includes assessments, direct therapy sessions, and caregiver training.
